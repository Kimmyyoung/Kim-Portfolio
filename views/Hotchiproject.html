Packaging Design

HOTCHI Packaging Design

Timeline January 2022 -
March 2022
Role Graphic Designer
Tools Adobe Illustrator CS6 Adobe Photoshop CS6
Project Overview

This project is to create a brand identity for a Kimchi company. The brand logo and business card based on client’s needs by engaging with a client in the design process.

01

Plan

I had a meeting with client to better understand client’s needs. I spent some time to reserch some competitor’s design through Pinterest, Behance etc. Also, the client confirmed main color palette and we decided to theme for overall design.

02

Design

Based on client’s needs and requests, I thought about some ideas and sketched different versions of packaging design and logo design on the paper. After colouring my sketch, I present them to the client to let her choose the best one.

03

Implement

After finishing all of the design tasks, my client needed to advertise our packaging to people. We decided to make a marketing plan through SNS which is Instagram and Facebook. I designed posts regarding packaging and we commit some time to advertise them. In the end, we made a contract to export items to the local market. The package and posts I designed contributed to an increase of 200% in sales of the company compared to last year.
